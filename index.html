Browse GWAS associations for viral infections and immune responses. Toggle between all associated SNPs (-- rows) or independent lead SNPs (-- loci after LD clumping).

Data Source: NHGRI-EBI GWAS Catalog v1.0.2 (Cerezo et al., Nucleic Acids Research, 2025, 53(D1):D998–D1005; doi:10.1093/nar/gkae1070). LD clumping performed using 1000 Genomes Project reference data (1000 Genomes Project Consortium, Nature, 2015, 526(7571):68–74; doi:10.1038/nature15393) with multi-population approach (EUR, AFR, EAS, AMR, SAS). Data filtered for P-value ≤ 5×10⁻⁸ and virus-related traits.

⚠️ Disclaimer: This tool provides mere exploratory analysis based on published GWAS associations. Results are subject to important limitations: (1) GWAS identify statistical associations in large populations, not deterministic genetic risks; (2) Imperfect linkage disequilibrium correction means SNPs may proxy for unmeasured variants; (3) Transferability across populations varies, so associations identified in one ancestry may not apply to others; (4) Risk stratification is preliminary and not a substitute for clinical assessment or medical advice. Users should interpret results cautiously and consult healthcare professionals for medical decisions.
